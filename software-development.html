Some Of Our Key Projects

Accelerating Incident Resolution with AI-Powered Incident Management System

We developed a state-of-the-art Integrated Command & Control Center (ICCC) combined with a post-incident debriefing software, IDA, for a leading renewable energy and infrastructure provider. Powered by IoT analytics and real-time insights, this solution has transformed incident detection, accelerated response times, and enhanced situational awareness—ensuring seamless management of critical infrastructure.

Enhancing Methods To Predict Natural Disasters With An Advanced Landslide Warning System

We developed an AI-powered Landslide Warning System across India’s most vulnerable regions, integrating real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and a 24/7 forecasting center. Leveraging cutting-edge ICT, the system ensures scalability, reliability, and rapid alerts, strengthening disaster preparedness and response.
