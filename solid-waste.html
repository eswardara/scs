Solid Waste Management

Modernize operations. Maximize visibility. Transform solid waste into a streamlined, data-driven process.

With rapid urbanization and rising waste volumes, municipalities and urban bodies are under pressure to deliver cleaner cities, faster response times, and sustainable operations. Traditional waste management models fall short without visibility, coordination, and data-backed planning.

At SCS Tech, we bring structure and intelligence to solid waste management—digitizing the entire chain from collection to disposal. Leveraging IoT, GIS, AI/ML, and real-time dashboards, we help authorities track assets, optimize collection routes, monitor infrastructure health, and ensure regulatory compliance. Our integrated platform improves operational transparency, reduces inefficiencies, and supports smarter urban governance. With SCS Tech, cities gain control over complexity—delivering cleaner environments and measurable impact.

Building cleaner, more efficient cities with connected waste management systems.

Intelligent Route Planning & Optimization

Optimize collection schedules using GIS and AI to reduce fuel consumption, avoid redundancies, and improve service coverage.

Real-Time Fleet Monitoring

Track vehicle location, speed, and route compliance through GPS-enabled dashboards for improved accountability and faster response.

Citizen Engagement & Grievance Redressal

Enable digital complaint systems with real-time updates, improving transparency and service feedback loops.

Compliance Monitoring & Environmental Reporting

Ensure adherence to waste handling norms with automated logs, emissions tracking, and regulatory-ready reports.
