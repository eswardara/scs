GIS-based Workforce & Fleet Tracking System

In complex field operations, visibility is the only way to stay in control.

When operations span cities, terrains, or entire states, knowing where, when, and how your workforce and assets move is non-negotiable. The platform enables centralized monitoring and real-time decision-making, integrated with integrating weighbridges, smart GPS devices, mobile apps, and citizen interfaces. It powers faster pickups, better route planning, and accurate SLA compliance. With real-time dashboards powered by GPS, smart wearables, and geo-tagged data, every on-ground action becomes visible, accountable, and optimizable.

Track every move, optimize every mile, deliver every outcome

Operational inefficiencies often hide in movement logs, delayed decisions, and missed opportunities. Our system captures and transforms this chaos — layering time-stamped actions, real-time alerts, digital logs, behavioral analytics, and location intelligence into one unified operational view. What you get: optimized performance, transparent workflows, and total field accountability.

Built for the demands of the field, designed for smart governance.

Already deployed across a metropolitan city, our solution is designed to scale. Whether you're managing 10 field teams or 10,000, it integrates seamlessly with your existing ERP, CRM, mobile apps, control rooms, and weighbridges — empowering municipalities to run data-driven, transparent, and efficient city-wide operations.

What Does the GIS Tracking System Actually Do ?

Locates & Monitors in Real-Time

From fleets to field crews, monitor movement with precision across GIS maps.

Optimizes Routes & Resources

Dynamic rerouting and resource allocation to reduce delays and fuel costs.

Improves SLA Compliance

Geo-fenced alerts, task tracking, and exception reports to boost service accountability.

Integrates Seamlessly

Easily integrates with ERP, CRM, mobile apps, and incident management platforms.
