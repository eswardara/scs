Robotic Processing Automation

As one of the leading providers of robotic process automation services in India, SCS Tech India Pvt Ltd brings over 15 years of proven expertise in driving operational efficiency and digital transformation. We've helped businesses across industries eliminate inefficiencies, reduce manual effort, and automate mission-critical processes with precision. Our intelligent RPA solutions streamline repetitive, rule-based tasks—delivering measurable gains in speed, accuracy, and cost efficiency. From deploying enterprise-grade bots to building AI-powered digital workforces, we create automation frameworks that integrate seamlessly with existing systems. Our work spans sectors like finance, manufacturing, logistics, and customer service—accelerating workflows, boosting compliance, and enabling clients to scale smarter and faster.

Case Study

Automating Urban Sustainability with Solid Waste Management Solution

We reengineered solid waste management for a Tier-1 city, implementing a GPS and GIS-enabled tracking system. Our solution automates waste collection, optimizes routes, integrates weighbridges, and enhances fleet management, driving efficiency, transparency, and sustainability.

Enhancing Methods To Predict Natural Disasters With An Advanced Landslide Warning System

We deployed a GIS and AI-powered Landslide Warning System across India’s most vulnerable regions—integrating spatial intelligence, real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and a 24/7 forecasting center. Enabled by advanced ICT, the system delivers rapid alerts with high scalability and reliability, enhancing disaster readiness and accelerating response.
